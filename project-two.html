Rain and Friends

The Rain and Friends game is the result of prototyping specially adjusted to a young target group. The goal was to involve audio and dynamic behaivor in such a way that, with the final prototype, keep younger players interested and motivated.

Context

9 week cours in dynamic prototyping at the Department of Computer and System Siences, DSV, Stockholm University.

Goal

The goals was to practice the development of dynamic prototypes.

Core values

The scope of the course did not include full-bodied user research, instead a couple of scientific articles were read to form an estimate of the mental capacity of a preschool child. Based on this research some core values was identified:

• kids around the age of 5 are eager to learn things they find engaging

• an environment without disturbing elements is crucial

• dynamic behaviour such as sound or movement (animation) can extend the attention span

Persona

The persona was composed based on the research made and the core values.

The created persona.

The solution

After elaborating on the needs and interests of the user group the idea of a game focused on how to learn mathematics was chosen. The goal was to involv as many senses as possible and to make it motivating to play, but also to not make it too komplex.

Flow charts

As a part of the development a couple of flow charts were made to get a better understandig of how the game could be constructed to fit the user group.
